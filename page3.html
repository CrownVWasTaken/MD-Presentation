Surprising or interesting things about Muscular Dystrophy

By the time they are thirty years old, almost everyone with Emery-Dreifuss MD has a cardiac condition. In addition to heart problems, female carriers of the condition frequently experience muscle weakness. Years before muscle weakening, the heart symptoms may in certain situations be the first and most prominent sign of the illness.
